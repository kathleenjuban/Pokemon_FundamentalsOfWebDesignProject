Oak's information:

Hello Adventurer!
My name is Professor Oak and welcome to our pokemon showcase. Here we have a sample of some of the pokemon that you will find out in the wild. If you are interested in becoming a pokemon trainer you are allowed to brouse our showcase to gain information. This information is vital to trying to catch one so read carefully!

The Fire type (Japanese: ほのおタイプ Flame type) is one of the eighteen types. all damaging Fire-type moves were special,but they may now also be physical depending on the attack. The Fire type grants immunity to burns and the sea of fire caused by Grass Pledge and Fire Pledge.

The Water type (Japanese: みずタイプ Water type) is one of the eighteen types. Water-type moves were special, but they may now also be physical depending on the attack. Water-type Pokémon fare well defensively, being weak to only Electric and Grass-type moves.

The Grass type (Japanese: くさタイプ Grass type) is one of the eighteen types. Grass-type moves were special, but they may now also be physical depending on the attack. Grass types are tied with Rock in having the most weaknesses out of all types with five.
