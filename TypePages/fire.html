Charmander

Charmander is a bipedal, reptilian Pokémon with a primarily orange body and blue eyes which is found in Kanto Region

Charmander can be found in hot, mountainous area and exhibits pack behavior, calling others of its species if it finds food.

Cyndaquil

Cyndaquil is a small, bipedal Pokémon with flames in its back which is found in Johto Region.

It is capable of defending itself using these flames, and the fire grows more powerful as it becomes angry or defensive. It is usually timid by nature and often curls into a ball when intimidated.

Torchic

Torchic is a small, chick Pokémon with stubby, downy, yellow wings which is found in Hoenn Region.

Somewhere in its belly, this Pokémon has a place where it keeps a flame. This internal flame causes Torchic to feel warm if hugged. When attacked, it will shoot fireballs of 1,000 °C at its foes, leaving them scorched black.

Chimchar

Chimchar is a bipedal Pokémon resembling a chimpanzee which is found in Sinnoh Region.

Its rear has a small, round, red patch that is usually obscured by flames produced by burning gas in its stomach. These flames go out when it goes to sleep, and burn weakly when it feels ill.

Fennekin

Fennekin is a small, quadrupedal fox-like Pokémon which is found in Kalos Region.

Hot air is vented from Fennekin's ears, reaching temperatures of over 200 °C. It uses this ability to intimidate opponents. Fennekin chews on twigs, which fill it with energy. It can be temperamental, but it tries to do its best for its Trainer.

Scorbunny

Scorbunny is a rabbit-like Pokémon which is found in Galar Region

Scorbunny loves to run and is filled with energy. A warm-up run can cause the fire energy in Scorbunny to start coursing through its body, allowing it to fight at full power. The special pads on the back of its feet and nose can generate heat once it is ready to fight.

Tepig

Tepig is a quadrupedal, pig-like Pokémon which is found in Unova Region

Tepig is a nimble Pokémon capable of blowing fire from its snout. It will cloak itself in these flames in order to use its former signature move, Heat Crash. However, it will spout thick, black smoke when ill.

Litten

Litten is a quadrupedal, feline Pokémon found in the Alola Region.

Litten is a solitary Pokémon that does not typically display its emotions, and should not be given too much affection. While grooming, it collects fur inside its stomach. By setting the stored fur alight, it can spit fireballs that change based on how it coughs.

Arceus

Arceus is known as "The Original One", as it is said that it created Sinnoh and Ransei, and possibly the entire Pokémon universe, along with the lake guardians and creation trio.

While it is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon, Arceus will change type when it is holding a Plate or type-specific Z-Crystal.
