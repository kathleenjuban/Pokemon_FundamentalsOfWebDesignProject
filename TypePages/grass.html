Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a small, quadrupedal Pokémon that can be found in the Kanto Region

On Bulbasaur's back is a green plant bulb, which is grown from a seed planted there at birth. The bulb also conceals two slender, tentacle-like vines and provides it with energy through photosynthesis as well as from the nutrient-rich seeds contained within.

Chikorita

Chikorita is a small, pale-green, quadrupedal Pokémon which is found in the John Region

A soothing aroma wafts from the leaf, which can calm battling Pokémon. The leaf is also used to sense temperature and humidity. Chikorita actively seeks out and enjoys the sun's warmth.

Treecko

Treecko is a small, green, bipedal reptilian Pokémon found in the Hoenn Region

It makes its home in tall trees and will attack anyone that comes near its nest. Because of its territorial nature, it is known to be the protector of the forest's trees.

Turtwig

Turtwig is a small, light green, quadrupedal Pokémon resembling a turtle or tortoise and is found in the Sinnoh Region

Since it is a plant-based Pokémon, Turtwig usually nourishes itself through photosynthesis. It also drinks water, which hardens its soil-based shell.

Chespin

Chespin is a bipedal, mammalian Pokémon found in the Kalos Region

Its shell can protect it from powerful attacks. The quills on its head are usually soft but can be stiffened for attacking as well. Despite having a curious nature that tends to get it in trouble, Chespin keeps an optimistic outlook and does not worry about small details.

Grookey

Grookey is a small, monkey-like Pokémon found in the Galar Region

Grookey has two, leaf-shaped hair tufts that hold a stick on the top of its head. Besides using it when attacking, Grookey beats this stick rhythmically against the surfaces of all sorts of objects

Rowlet

Rowlet is a small, avian Pokémon resembling a young owl with a round body and short legs which is found in the Hisui Region

As a nocturnal Pokémon, it stores energy during the day through photosynthesis. It approaches opponents by silently gliding, before unleashing a volley of kicks.

Snivy

Snivy is a bipedal, reptilian Pokémon with a slender build which is found in the Unova Region

This Pokémon has a calm, collected demeanor.

Arceus

Arceus is known as "The Original One", as it is said that it created Sinnoh and Ransei, and possibly the entire Pokémon universe, along with the lake guardians and creation trio.

While it is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon, Arceus will change type when it is holding a Plate or type-specific Z-Crystal.
