Squirtle

Squirtle is a small Pokémon that resembles a light-blue turtle which is found in the Kanto Region.

Squirtle's shell is a useful tool. It can withdraw into the shell for protection or to sleep.

Totodile

Totodile is a bipedal, crocodilian Pokémon with well-developed jaws which is found in the Johto Region.

Totodile tends to be playful by nature and has a habit of biting anything it sees, including its Trainer.

Mudkip

Mudkip is a small, amphibious, quadrupedal Pokémon which is found in Hoenn Region.

It is a very docile Pokémon and sleeps by burying itself in the soil at the water's edge.

Piplup

Piplup is a penguin-like Pokémon, which is covered in thick down to insulate against the cold and is found in Sinnoh Region.

Piplup has a strong sense of self-pride. Because of its pride, it has a hard time accepting food from humans and bonding with its caretakers.

Oshawott

Oshawott is a bipedal sea otter-like Pokémon which is found in Hisui Region.

It carries a pale yellow seashell called a "scalchop" on its belly. This shell is made of the same material as its claws, and is used for both battle and cracking hard berries.

Froakie

Froakie is a quadrupedal, frog-like Pokémon which is found in Kalos Region.

Despite its carefree attitude, it is vigilant of its surroundings. Froakie is both light and strong, making it capable of jumping incredibly high.

Popplio

Popplio is a pinniped Pokémon that is primarily blue which is found in Alola Region.

Popplio is able to snort bubbles of water from its nose, which it uses as part of its battle strategy. On land, it uses the elasticity of its bubbles to perform acrobatic stunts and jumps.

Sobble

Sobble is a small Pokémon that has light-blue skin with darker teal patches and a lighter blue belly and face which is found in Galar Region.

Sobble has the ability to camouflage itself in water where it hides itself. When it touches the water, Sobble's body changes its patterns and colors. This allows Sobble to blend into its surroundings.

Arceus

Arceus is known as "The Original One", as it is said that it created Sinnoh and Ransei, and possibly the entire Pokémon universe, along with the lake guardians and creation trio.

While it is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon, Arceus will change type when it is holding a Plate or type-specific Z-Crystal.
