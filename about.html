About

The World of Pokemon is an informational website on Pokemon which is written by a group of three students (Eduardo Amorim, Henry Gushurst, and Kathleen Juban) currently studying at New Brunswick Community College.

The website has categorized the Pokemon characters into three major types, mainly, introducing the characters and taking you along with their evolutions and locations.

Currently studying at Saint John, New Brunswick, Canada 😀
